This is what we do.

We at SunsetPicnic are always at the cutting edge of software development without sacrificing the experience of the past years. We see ourselves as a network of experts around the globe who want to push the world forward. We believe in the uniqueness of each project and would like to share our knowledge with you.

  • Software Engineering

    We develop amazing web and mobile applications for your needs. Our key technologies are here: Angular and React in the frontend. Go, Nodejs and Rust in the backend. We love React Native for mobile apps. Test-driven software development and scalability are as much in focus as security.

  • Consulting & Training

    We share knowledge with your team. Our lecturers are all experienced software developers who have already worked on complex problems. Our training courses are didactically tested and follow a learning by doing approach - the ideal introduction to a new technology for your next project.

  • AI & Machine Learning

    Hardly any other development stimulates our imagination like artificial intelligence and machine learning. We have the declared goal to play a leading role in this development. In particular, we rely on TensorFlow with Keras and PyTorch, as well as OpenCV for Computer Vision. Let your software solve problems for you and your customers in an intelligent way.

  • UX & Product

    Every technical project is unique. It would be a pity if it is rejected by the user because of its appearance. Understandable, after all a design language plays an essential role in the psychology of humans. We at SunsetPicnic focus on the User Experience (UX) from the very beginning and help you in product development, iteratively and in cooperation with you.

Sustainable Energy

The spread of renewable energies is a central pillar of the energy system transformation. The growth of renewable energies is particularly in the interest of climate and environmental protection in order to develop a sustainable energy supply. In addition, the economic costs of energy supply are to be reduced, fossil energy resources conserved and technological development in the field of renewable energies promoted.
